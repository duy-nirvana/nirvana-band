STORY OF

NIRVANA

Nirvana was an American rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington, in 1987. It was founded by lead singer and guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic . Nirvana went through a succession of drummers, the longest-lasting and best-known being Dave Grohl .
